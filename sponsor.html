Sponsorships

#a11yTO has been creating, supporting, and expanding the digital accessibility community in and around Toronto for over 5 years. #a11yTO believes in introducing digital accessibility to new people all year round through our monthly Meetups and at Camp. This year we’re riding the crest of our inaugural #a11yTO conference and we want to build on the momentum. #a11yTOConf was the pinnacle event of 2017 and we’re already planning our move to the next level and making 2018 #a11yTO’s most impactful year ever.

None of this would be possible without your support. Your sponsorship will go towards the costs for American Sign Language (ASL), live captioning, logistics, food, and supplies for #a11yTOCamp and our 2018 Meetups.

Your donations will help to support Camp first and foremost, but they will continue to fund our Meetups, socials and operations for the entire calendar year.
