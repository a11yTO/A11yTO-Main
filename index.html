#a11yWeekTO Kickoff

We're excited to announce #a11yWeekTO Kickoff!

Join us on Tuesday, October 19, 2021, at 6 pm EST as your #a11yWeekTO hosts Oskar, Billy, Jennifer, and Steve officially launch our week of events.

The #a11yTO gang will run down all the #a11yWeekTO events, the talks they're looking forward to, and they may even have a couple of surprises in store.

#a11yWeekTO Kickoff is 100% free and open to anyone who wishes to attend, join us as we launch #a11yWeekTO

If you have tickets for any of our events, you can also watch via the #a11yTO Event portal.

Register for one or all of our scheduled October events!

Our week-long event features:

  • #a11yTO Conf (our digital accessibility conference);
  • #a11yIRL (our Accessibility in Real Life event), and;
  • #a11yTO Gaming (our accessible gaming conference)
