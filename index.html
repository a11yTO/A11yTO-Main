#a11yWeekTO

October 19-24, 2020

#a11yWeekTO is back for a second year

After a successful first year, the #a11yTO Team is thrilled announce the return of #a11yWeekTO!

This week-long celebration of accessibilty features our core events: #a11yTO Conf (our digital accessibility conference), #a11yIRL (Accessibility In Real Life - our built environment conference), and #a11yTO Gaming (our accessible gaming conference), in addition to several soon-to-be-announced day and evening events.

It has long been our goal to make Toronto a destination where we can showcase the depth and diversity of our accessibility community. Last year we welcomed hundreds of speakers and attendees from all over the world, and look forward to doing it all again in October, 2020!

  • #a11yIRL - Wednesday, October 21
  • #a11yTO Conf - Thursday, October 22 and Friday, October 23
  • #a11yTO Gaming - Saturday, October 24

Save the date! More #a11yWeekTO events and ticket info will be announced in the new year.

If you are interested in sponsoring #a11yWeekTO or any of its events, please get in touch with us at sponsor@a11yto.com.
