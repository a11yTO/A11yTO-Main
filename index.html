#a11yWeekTO

With #a11yTO Conf, #a11yIRL and the addition of #a11yTO Gaming, #a11yTO has finalized our core schedule of events for the first annual #a11yWeekTO! It has long been our goal to make Toronto a destination where we can showcase the depth and diversity of our accessibility community. With these core events, and other soon-to-be-announced evening events, we are excited to host attendees and presenters from around the world.

The current #a11yWeekTO schedule is as follows:
