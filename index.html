Home

#a11yTO actively promotes digital inclusion for the design and development communities in and around the Greater Toronto Area (GTA). We welcome people of all abilities and experience - from people curious about what this accessibility thing is all about to experts in the field.

#a11yTO holds regular Meetups, an annual Camp, and Conferences too! Our annual Conference, of course, plus 2019 brings the addition of #a11yIRL, focused specifically on the accessibility of the built environment, and #a11yTO Gaming, an event dedicated to accessible gaming. We'd love to meet you at our next event!

#a11yWeekTO

With #a11yTO Conf, #a11yIRL and the addition of #a11yTO Gaming, #a11yTO has finalized our core schedule of events for the first annual #a11yWeekTO! It has long been our goal to make Toronto a destination event where we can showcase the depth and diversity of our accessibility community. With these core events, and other soon to be announced evening events, we are excited to host attendees and presenters from around the world.

The current #a11yWeekTO schedule is as follows:

  • October 23 - #a11yIRL
  • October 24 & 25 - #a11yTO Conf
  • October 26 - #a11yTO Gaming
