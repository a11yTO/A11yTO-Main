Meetups

Our goal in hosting our meetups is to provide a place for you to learn, share your experiences, ideas, and solutions and collaborate on building a more inclusive web.

If you're interested in accessibility and inclusive design, join us. We try to meet once a month for presentations, tech talks, demos and more. We post updates on our Twitter account, @a11yTO, be sure to follow us there for the latest news.
