Thank you for joining us for our first Audio Described Haunted Ghost walk with storyteller and Audio Describer J.J. Hunt. The guided walking tour of Toronto’s most haunted buildings featured tales of murder, ghosts, and spirits, teeming with visual description, sure to terrify Blind and Sighted guests alike.

#a11yTO Gaming

#a11yTO Gaming is back!

We are pleased to announce that #a11yTO Gaming is returning as an in-person event this October! To celebrate our return, we’ve completely reimagined the format and incorporated a more “activity focused” approach to this year’s event!

This means in addition to talks from industry leaders and needle-movers, we are also including multiple installations including: an accessible gaming arcade, QA sessions with experts, live gaming demos, and much much more to be announced in the coming weeks!

Tickets are on sale now for the early bird price of $49! Get yours today!

Meetups

Our goal in hosting our meetups is to provide a place for you to learn, share your experiences, ideas, and solutions and collaborate on building a more inclusive web.

If you're interested in accessibility and inclusive design, join us. We try to meet once a month for presentations, tech talks, demos and more. We post updates on our Twitter account, @a11yTO, be sure to follow us there for the latest news.

#a11yTO Camp
