Official Kickoff!

We’re excited to announce the final addition to our #a11yWeekTO series of events… the #a11yWeekTO Kickoff! Join us on Tuesday, October 20, 2020 at 6pm EST as your #a11yTO Conf hosts Oskar Westin and Billy Gregory officially launch our week of events.

In addition to previewing #a11yTO Conf, #a11yIRL, and #a11yTO Gaming, the #a11yWeekTO Kickoff will feature talks by Lainey Feingold, and Adrian Roselli, and we'll be hearing from #a11yIRL Sarah Manteuffel #a11yTO Gaming host Steve Saylor.

The Kick-off event is 100% free and open to anyone who wishes to attend, join us to help launch #a11yWeekTO. If you have tickets for any of our events you can also watch via the #a11yTO Event portal.

#a11yWeekTO

The #a11yTO Team is thrilled to announce that #a11yWeekTO is going to be 100% virtual this year!

Our week-long event features: #a11yTO Conf (our digital accessibility conference), #a11yIRL (our Accessibility in Real Life event), and #a11yTO Gaming (our accessible gaming conference).

Toronto has depth and diversity within our accessibility community. While we can’t all be together in person this year, we are still committed to showcasing what our community has to offer. Last year we welcomed hundreds of speakers and attendees from all over the world.
