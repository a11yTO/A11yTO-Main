#a11yWeekTO

With #a11yTO Conf, #a11yIRL and the addition of #a11yTO Gaming, #a11yTO has finalized our schedule of events for the first annual #a11yWeekTO! It has long been our goal to make Toronto a destination where we can showcase the depth and diversity of our accessibility community. With these core events, and other soon-to-be-announced evening events, we are excited to host attendees and presenters from around the world.

#a11yWeekTO returns! October 19-24, 2020!

  • #a11yIRL - Wednesday October 21
  • #a11yTOConf - October 22nd and 23rd
  • #a11yTOGaming - Saturday October 24th

... more #a11yWeekTO events to be announced.
